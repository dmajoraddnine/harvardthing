Annual Report

The Annual Report, or Status Report, is for existing Grantees to update their progress toward publication. An annual report is due each year until the final publication of your project. Annual Reports from current Grantees are due by January 15, 2014. In addition to the annual report, if you would like to continue to recieve funding, you must also send a Renewal Request. If you have questions or concern, please email White Levy or give us a call at 617-495-9317.

NOTE: If you received a new grant in 2012, your first Annaul Report and Renewal Request is due August 1st, 2013. You should have recieved this notice about the timeline change in your contract.

To complete your annual report, please send one email containing the following items to: whitelev@fas.harvard.edu

1. Status Report

 PDF or word document you create, named "yourname_annualreport.doc"

2. Related Images, Maps, Graphs/Charts

 up to 3 images, named "yourname_image1", "yourname_image2", etc. If you would like to make one PDF which includes your Status Report and related images, that is also fine.

And if you have had a change in address (home or office) since your last report, please fill out the grantee information PDF


1. Status Report

Your status report should include details of the status of your project, your progress toward publication, and accounting of your funds. It may also include samples of work to date (sample chapter, data sheet, drawings, etc.).

FILE NAME: Your application should be named for the archaeologist/grantee and include the word "report" in the name. For example, the file might be named "JoeSmith_annualreport"

FILE FORMAT: Your application should be a PDF (prefered) or a Word compatible document.

 

2. Images, Maps, Graphs/Charts

It is optional for you to submit images as a part of your report, although you are strongly encouraged to do so. You may either submit them seperatley, or make one PDF that includes your Status Report and the images. If you submit them seperately:

FILE NAME: All of your files should include your name as part of the file name.

FILE FORMAT: Ideally, images should be .jpeg or .tiff., approximately 800 x 500 pixels, with a resolution of 72 dpi. If the images are larger, you may placed them a single folder and zip them.

CAPTIONS: We encourage you to include a PDF or word file as a part of this submission which has captions for the images.
