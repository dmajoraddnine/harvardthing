Renewal Request

In addition to an Annual Report, grantees who would like their funding to continue must report to the Board each year to request the renewal of their funds. Successful reporting will lead to the renewal of the grant for another year.

If it is the first year of your grant in 2012, your Renewal Request is due on August 1st, 2013
If it is the second or third year of your grant, your Renewal is due January 22, 2014

 

Please email a PDF or word compatable document containing

  • Name of grantee
  • Current address, phone number(s), fax, and email information
  • Summary of work completed to date
  • Outline of work to be completed
  • Accounting of grant money received to date
  • 2013 budget with specific money request in US dollars
  • A request to renew your fundng for an additional year

    • to whitelev@fas.harvard.edu. Please name your document "yourname_renewal".
